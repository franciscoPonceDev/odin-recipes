Rice

Description:

Simple rice recipe seasoned with garlic and salt.

Ingredients:

  • 1 and a half tablespoon of soybean oil
  • 2 Garlic cloves
  • 1 and a half teacup of rice
  • 3 teacups of water
  • 2 coffee-spoon of salt

Steps:

  1. Mash the garlic together with the salt until it becomes a homogeneous mash
  2. In a soup pot, add the oil, the rice and the mashed garlic. Cook it mixing for approximately two minutes.
  3. Add the water and mix a little.
  4. Lower the heat, partially cover half of the soup pot and cook until the rice is dry.
  5. Turn off the heat, cover the pot for another 5 minutes so that the rice finishes cooking in its own steam.
